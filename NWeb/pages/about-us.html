About N-Web Plus

It was registered in The Gambia in 2007 with the intention of providing innovative ICT solutions. We have been in business for a number of years providing a range of IT solutions namely Website development, Web hosting, Antivirus & Email Solutions, IT Audit, Software Development, PC Hardware Repairs, Network Installation and Support, IT hardware and software procurement.

Amongst our success stories has been our partnership with the Gambia Chamber of Commerce and Industry (GCCI) in 2012 and 2013 to provide live web streaming of the annual trade fair with great success and commendation. We have also been Co-founders of Innovative ICT Solutions in The Gambia such as FARM FRESH (www.farmfresh.gm – Gambia’s first online food store), GM JOBS (www.gamjobs.com, Gambia’s First Online Social Job website), JULABAA (www.julabaa.com – An innovative one-stop shop for all products).
